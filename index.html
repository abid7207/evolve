About EVOLVE 2025

EVOLVE is the flagship event of IEEE PES SBC JECC and IEEE SPS SBC JECC in collaboration with IEEE SB JECC, hosted at Jyothi Engineering College, Cheruthuruthy. This two-day event offers hands-on workshops, expert talks, competitions, and games, fostering innovation, learning, and networking among tech enthusiasts.

Why EVOLVE ?

EVOLVE is more than just an event—it’s a gateway to innovation, skill development, and industry connections. With workshops, expert talks, competitions, and networking opportunities, it empowers participants to stay ahead in technology and build a brighter, more informed future. Join us to learn, compete, and grow!

What awaits at EVOLVE ?

  • Workshops

    Engage in transformative, hands-on sessions designed to build real-world skills. Covering advanced topics like AI, smart grids, and cutting-edge technologies, these workshops offer a practical learning experience that equips participants to tackle industry challenges effectively.

  • Talks

    Listen to inspiring sessions by industry experts and thought leaders who share their knowledge on groundbreaking trends and innovations. Gain exclusive insights into the latest advancements and their practical applications in technology and engineering.

  • Games

    Experience fun-filled, interactive tech-themed games that combine learning and entertainment. From brain-teasing puzzles to thrilling challenges, these activities promise to spark creativity and foster teamwork in a relaxed environment.

  • Competitions

    Put your skills to the test in dynamic competitions that challenge your creativity, innovation, and technical expertise. Compete with the brightest minds to showcase your talent and win exciting rewards, all while expanding your network.
